UW Baby helps growing families prepare for baby

UW Baby, a pregnancy app ​

Read, watch and learn about baby

Find answers for your questions about what’s happening during pregnancy, week by week, customized to your due date. Watch informative videos to understand the birth process or how your body prepares for birth. Read articles on pregnancy, labor and birth, postpartum care, breastfeeding, newborn care. Find UW Medicine providers and clinics, and more.

Track appointments, baby kicks and more!

Capture every kick with our interactive tracker tools. You can record appointments, contractions, immunization records and more, during pregnancy and through the first year of childhood. UW Baby is your pregnancy and newborn care companion.
